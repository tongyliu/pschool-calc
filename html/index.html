Welcome

Ever wonder what will happen to your grade after your teacher puts the next assignment in? Maybe you bombed a test, and you want to see just how badly a 63% will bring down your grade. Or, maybe you did well, and you want to see how much a 99% will raise it.

If your school uses PowerSchool to manage grades, you're in luck! With this handy app, you can do exactly that!
